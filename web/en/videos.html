Thank you for joining us!

PyCon SK is a community-organized conference for the Python programming language. The inaugural PyCon SK was held in Bratislava on 11th - 13th March, 2016.

  • 26 speakers from ten countries
  • 400 attendees
  • 500 live stream watchers
